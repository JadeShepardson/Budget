Elephant

What is Elephant?

Hello. This is Elephant, a web app designed to help you budget. Using combinatorial optimization via the Knapsack Problem, Elephant can help determine how to best use your money.

How to Use Elephant?

To start, determine the weight of your "knapsack" or how much money you have for this period in the "Total Budget" box.

Next add in your expenses using the item, value, and weight boxes. The item is the expenese, the value is how important it is from 0 to 10 (1 being most important and 10 being least), and the weight is how much each item costs.

The submit button is for when you are ready so see your generated budget. The reset button resets your inputs. The more button gives you more items to add to your potential budget.
